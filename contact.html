What Happens When You Call?

No robotic phone trees, no “someone will call you back in three to five business days.” Here’s the actual process:

1

Quick Phone Call (5–10 min)

We ask about your floor type, square footage, and what you’re trying to accomplish. We’ll tell you right away if your project is a fit for us.

2

On-Site Visit & Moisture Test

We schedule a walkthrough to assess the concrete and test moisture vapor emission. This step is free and is what lets us give you an accurate quote—not a guess.

3

Written Estimate

You get a line-by-line estimate with the system type, number of coats, and total price. No hidden charges after the job starts.

4

Installation Day

Most Tucson residential jobs are done in one to two days. We handle prep, coating, and cleanup—you just need to clear the floor the night before.
